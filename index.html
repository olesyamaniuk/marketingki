Our Development Process

How We Build Complex Fun

Every Repairsabe game is built with modularity, testing, and iteration in mind. Our pipeline:

  • 1

    Idea Scaffold

    Mechanical and thematic concepts are sketched into modular components

  • 2

    Playable Systems

    Early sandbox models test how tools, puzzles, and interactions feel

  • 3

    Narrative Mesh

    Story is layered over tested gameplay to maintain flow and meaning

  • 4

    Test, Refine, Test Again

    We value feedback loops at every stage — from testers and AI-based UX analyzers

  • 5

    Post-Launch Support

    Our games grow with new content, system enhancements, and expansions
